The Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe makes delicious, chewy chocolate chip cookies that are perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1'2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually blend into the creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the pre heated oven.
  8. Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to the wire racks.
